Objective: 
To design and implement a smart shopping cart system using RFID technology, IoT integration, and cloud services that enables self‑checkout, eliminates long queues, and enhances the overall shopping experience for customers.

Problem Statement: 
Traditional retail checkout processes often involve long waiting lines, manual scanning of items, and dependency on cashiers. This leads to:

- Customer frustration and wasted time.
- Inefficient store operations.
- Limited scalability during peak shopping hours.

Purpose:
- Seamless self‑checkout without cashier intervention.
- Real‑time cart management (add/remove items instantly).
- Faster payment processing with contactless RFID cards.
- Improved customer satisfaction through reduced queues and convenience.
- Enterprise scalability with analytics, inventory tracking, and cloud‑based resilience.
